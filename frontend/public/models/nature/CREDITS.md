# Nature models

**Ultimate Stylized Nature** by **Quaternius** — CC0 (public domain).
https://quaternius.com · https://poly.pizza/u/Quaternius

Models converted from the pack's FBX to GLB (binary, embedded textures) with
[FBX2glTF](https://github.com/facebookincubator/FBX2glTF). Original albedo kept;
the NeuraClaw world lighting (day/night + weather + fog + bloom) sets the mood.

No attribution is required for CC0, but credit is given gladly.
